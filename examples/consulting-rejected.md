# Example — Strategy Consulting, REJECTED

A complete screening run. All names and details are fictional.

---

## Input 1 — Job Description (pasted by the user)

> **Junior Consultant — Milan Office**
> **Athena Strategy Partners** (boutique strategy firm, ~200 consultants across Europe)
>
> We are looking for a Junior Consultant to join our Milan office, working on strategy and transformation engagements for clients in financial services and consumer goods.
>
> **Responsibilities**
> - Conduct market sizing, benchmarking, and competitor analyses
> - Build financial models and business cases supporting client recommendations
> - Prepare client-ready deliverables (slide decks, steering committee materials)
> - Work directly with client teams and present findings to senior stakeholders
>
> **Requirements**
> - Master's degree in Economics, Engineering, or Business from a top university
> - 1–3 years of experience in consulting, corporate strategy, or a highly analytical role
> - Advanced Excel and PowerPoint; financial modeling experience
> - Fluent English and Italian
> - Strong structured problem-solving and communication skills
